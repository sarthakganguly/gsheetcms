Let the Mute Witnesses Speak


The list of all temples, stupas and monuments that were destroyed is mentioned herein. Some of them may be replaced by masjids, some may remain in various stages of ruin while some may even be reclaimed by the Dharmic community. In case the authenticity of a particular claim in the list below is considered questionable, it is marked as such. The references for each of these monuments will be provided soon.

The name of the page (and this web page in entirety) is inspired by the original works of Sita Ram Goel, especially the chapter 'Let the Mute Witnesses Speak' from the book 'Hindu Temples: What Happened to Them'.
